Editorial note
MR
Maya Reyes
Senior editor

Most "backpack essentials" lists are too generous — every charger, every pouch, every notebook gets a slot. Ours is the opposite. The bar to be on this page is simple: does it survive a normal corporate week without becoming clutter?

What you'll find here is what made the cut after months of rotation between offices, airports, client sites, and one cramped coworking spot in Lisbon. Items are tiered by how often we'd recommend them — not how shiny they are.
