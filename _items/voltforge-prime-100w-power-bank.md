## Verdict

The Voltforge Prime 100W Power Bank is travel power for days when outlets are
not dependable. It is too much battery for a normal office day and exactly the
kind of backup that makes a delayed flight less dangerous to the calendar.

## Best for

Use it for client weeks, flights, hotel desks, and long days away from
reliable power. It belongs with a serious travel bag, not a light office sling.

## What it solves

The problem is a schedule that outlasts the wall outlets. Airports are crowded,
conference rooms are stingy, and hotel desks often hide the only plug behind
furniture. A 100W bank turns that from a work stoppage into a manageable delay.

## What worked

The output is high enough to support a laptop power plan, not just phones and
earbuds. The 348 g weight is noticeable, but acceptable when the day includes
travel transitions and uncertain rooms.

## What did not

It is dead weight if your office has reliable outlets. Carrying it every day
because it might be useful is how bags become heavier without becoming better.

## Final call

Pack it for travel and client weeks. Leave it behind for normal office days.
Good power backup should reduce anxiety, not become its own weight penalty.
