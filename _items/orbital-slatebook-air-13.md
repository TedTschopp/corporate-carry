## Verdict

The 13 inch Orbital Slatebook Air is the light daily laptop profile in Maya's
office-day kit. It defines the carry math for a small bag setup where
portability matters more than a large screen.

## Best for

Use this profile for hybrid work, train commutes, meeting rooms, and office
days where portability matters more than a large screen. At 1.24 kg, it keeps
the rest of the bag from starting behind.

## What it solves

The failure is treating every workday like a heavy workstation day. A lighter
laptop makes the sling or compact totepack possible, which changes the whole
carry system.

## What worked

The weight is the story. A 1.24 kg laptop leaves room for a stand, charger,
pouch, notebook, and pen without making the office-day bag feel overloaded.

## What did not

It is not the right reference point for heavy creative work, large spreadsheets,
or client weeks that need more screen and more power. That is where the 16 inch
Pro profile belongs.

## Final call

Use it as the light-laptop baseline. The smaller the computer, the more honest
the rest of the carry has to be.
