## Verdict

The Rellan Safari Rollerball is the pen for people who want a meeting tool,
not a desk ritual. It writes immediately, looks professional without trying too
hard, and is refillable enough to stay in the kit after the first cartridge.

## Best for

Use it for client notes, meeting markups, and daily writing in a notebook like
the Northlamp 1917 Hardcover A5. It is especially good when the pen may be
handed across a table and needs to look competent without becoming a topic.

## What it solves

The failure mode is the pouch pen that does not write when the room gets
quiet. A reliable rollerball removes that tiny embarrassment and keeps the
meeting moving.

## What worked

The 18 g weight is light enough for a meeting pouch, and the black rollerball
ink has the right default energy: clear, quick, and easy to read. The refillable
body also keeps it from becoming disposable clutter.

## What did not

The shape is specific. If narrow grips bother you, try before buying or choose
the simpler Rellan pen. Comfort matters more than brand loyalty.

## Final call

Carry one good pen. This is the one to choose when writing is a daily meeting
tool rather than an occasional signature.
