## Verdict

The Perchform Laptop Stand v3 is the hot-desk posture fix that earns its place
because it weighs 170 g and folds away when the desk disappears. It is not
exciting. That is one of its strengths.

## Best for

Use it for hot desks, hotel desks, office days, and any setup where the laptop
screen otherwise sits too low for more than an hour. It pairs best with an
external keyboard, but it can still help in shorter sessions without one.

## What it solves

The common failure is ergonomic drift. You arrive at a borrowed desk, open the
laptop, and spend the day folded toward the screen. A folding stand lifts the
screen quickly enough that setup does not become a project.

## What worked

The stand folds to a slim shape and packs cleanly beside a cable or notebook.
It turns a hotel desk into something more workable in under a minute and does
not demand a permanent desk footprint.

## What did not

The limitation is input. If you type on the laptop keyboard all day while it is
raised, your wrists will object. For full-day use, pair it with a keyboard and
pointing device.

## Final call

Buy it if you move between desks and keep noticing your shoulders by lunch.
Skip it if you already have a fixed monitor or refuse to carry external input
for longer sessions.
