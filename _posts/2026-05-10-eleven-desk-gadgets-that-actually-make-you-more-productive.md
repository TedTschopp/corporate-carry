<p class="lede">Desk upgrades get generous writeups because they photograph well. We used a less glamorous test: did the item still get used after week three, and did it reduce a real daily annoyance?</p>

<h2><span class="sec">01</span>What stayed</h2>
<p>The stand stayed. The hub stayed. The cable that did not kink stayed. The pouch stayed because a clean desk often starts inside the bag. Anything that required a new charging ritual, a proprietary app, or a second explanation to a coworker was removed.</p>

<h2><span class="sec">02</span>What we skipped</h2>
<p>We skipped gadgets that made the desk look smarter while making the workday heavier. A desk tool earns the spot when it shortens setup, reduces cable mess, or makes repeated work physically easier. Everything else is decor with a USB port.</p>
